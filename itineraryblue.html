Blue Plan Itinerary

Our no expenses spared plan's suggested itinerary. If you wish to create a custom itinerary, please contact us through the help page. Custom itineraries may cost more.

Blue Plan – 5-Day Itinerary

Day 1

7:00 am: Check-in at London St Pancras International (Eurostar Business Premier Lounge)

8:16 am: Eurostar departs to Amsterdam Centraal

11:12 am: Arrive at Amsterdam Centraal Station

11:30 am: Private luxury transfer to Waldorf Astoria Amsterdam

12:00 pm: Check-in and welcome drink at Waldorf Astoria Amsterdam

1:00 pm: Lunch at Peacock Alley within the hotel

3:00 pm: Private canal cruise with champagne and hors d'oeuvres

5:00 pm: Return to hotel for relaxation at the Guerlain Spa

7:30 pm: Dinner at Spectrum, the hotel's two Michelin-starred restaurant

Day 2

8:00 am: Breakfast at Peacock Alley

9:00 am: Private transfer to Rijksmuseum

9:30 am: Guided tour of Rijksmuseum

11:30 am: Walk to Van Gogh Museum

12:00 pm: Explore Van Gogh Museum

1:30 pm: Lunch at RIJKS®, Michelin-starred restaurant at the museum

3:00 pm: Return to hotel for leisure time or spa treatments

7:00 pm: Dinner at De Kas, a unique restaurant in a greenhouse setting

Day 3

8:00 am: Breakfast at the hotel

9:00 am: Private transfer to Anne Frank House

9:30 am: Guided tour of Anne Frank House

11:00 am: Coffee break at nearby café

12:00 pm: Return to hotel for light lunch

2:00 pm: Luxury VIP Bike Tour with chauffeur, exploring Amsterdam's highlights

4:00 pm: Return to hotel for relaxation

7:30 pm: Dinner at The Duchess, a Michelin-starred restaurant

Day 4

7:00 am: Early breakfast at the hotel

8:00 am: Private transfer to Zaanse Schans

9:00 am: Explore windmills and traditional crafts at Zaanse Schans

11:00 am: Transfer to Keukenhof Gardens

12:00 pm: Lunch at Restaurant De Vier Seizoenen near Keukenhof

1:30 pm: Stroll through Keukenhof Gardens

4:00 pm: Return to hotel

7:00 pm: Farewell dinner at Bord'Eau, a Michelin-starred restaurant

Day 5

8:00 am: Breakfast at the hotel

9:00 am: Check-out and private transfer to Amsterdam Centraal Station

11:15 am: Eurostar Business Premier departs to London

2:05 pm: Arrive at London St Pancras International
