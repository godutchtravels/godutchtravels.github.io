Blue Plan – 5-Day Itinerary

Day 1

7:30 am: Check in at Eurostar Terminal, London St. Pancras

8:16 am: Depart on Eurostar to Amsterdam Centraal (Standard Premier)

12:12 pm: Arrive at Amsterdam Centraal Station

12:30 pm: Private transfer to Pulitzer Amsterdam

1:00 pm: Check-in and unpack at Pulitzer Amsterdam

2:00 pm: Lunch at Jansz. (hotel restaurant)

3:30 pm: Relax and explore the Pulitzer gardens/play area

5:00 pm: Private family canal cruise departs from hotel dock

6:30 pm: Return from cruise, freshen up

7:30 pm: Dinner at De Kas (greenhouse restaurant)

9:30 pm: Return to hotel and rest

Day 2

7:30 am: Wake up and breakfast at Pulitzer Hotel

9:00 am: Taxi to Rijksmuseum

9:30 am: Explore Rijksmuseum (use family guide)

11:30 am: Walk to Van Gogh Museum

12:00 pm: Tour Van Gogh Museum

1:15 pm: Lunch at Museumplein café (e.g. Cobra Café)

2:15 pm: Tram to Vondelpark

2:30 pm: Leisure time in Vondelpark (playgrounds, relax)

4:00 pm: Ice cream break at Vondeltuin

5:00 pm: Return to hotel

6:30 pm: Dinner at Jansz. or room service

8:00 pm: Optional evening stroll along the canals

Day 3

7:00 am: Early breakfast at hotel

8:00 am: Depart via private transfer to Keukenhof Gardens

9:00 am: Explore Keukenhof Gardens (tulip displays, children's maze)

12:00 pm: Lunch at Keukenhof Pavilion café

1:00 pm: Transfer to Zaanse Schans

2:00 pm: Tour windmills, cheese farm, and clog workshop

4:30 pm: Return to Amsterdam

5:30 pm: Rest and freshen up at hotel

7:00 pm: Dinner at Moeders

Day 4

8:00 am: Breakfast at hotel

9:00 am: Visit Anne Frank House (pre-booked tickets)

10:30 am: Walk to The Pancake Bakery for brunch

11:30 am: Explore The Nine Streets shopping district

1:00 pm: Return to hotel to relax or visit a museum/play area if time allows

3:00 pm: Pack and check out

3:30 pm: Private transfer to Amsterdam Centraal

4:11 pm: Eurostar train departs Amsterdam

8:00 pm: Arrive back in London
