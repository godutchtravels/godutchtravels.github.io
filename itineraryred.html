Red Plan Itinerary

Our red plan is the cheapest of our plans and costs €1550 for a family of four. It is designed for younger children aged 4 - 8 and includes visits to parks, Nemo Science museum and Pancake Boat Amsterdam, a tour of Dutch canals with all you can eat pancakes. The Red Plan accomodation is the Amadi Panorama Hotel, a family-friendly hotel near to Ijburg Bay. The itinerary below can be customised by contacting us through our help page (custom itineraries may cost more).

Red Plan – 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1

7:30 am: Check in to Eurostar

8:00 am: Eurostar departs

12:00 pm: Arrive in Amsterdam

12:30 pm: Take tram to Amadi Panorama Hotel and unpack

12:40 pm: Eat lunch and unwind at Amadi Panorama Hotel

1:30 pm: Walk down to the beach

3:00 pm: Enjoy the beach until 3:00 pm

3:50 pm: Take tram to Amsterdam Pancake Boat

4:00 pm: Board Pancake Boat

5:15 pm: Disembark from Pancake Boat and take tram back to hotel

6:00 pm: Relax and eat dinner at Amadi Panorama Hotel

Day 2

7:00 am: Wake up, eat breakfast at the hotel and get ready for a new day in Amsterdam

8:00 am: Leave hotel to visit Jeugdland

8:45 am: Arrive at Jeugdland

11:00 am: Leave Jeugdland

11:45 pm: Arrive at Artis Royal Zoo

12:00 pm: Enter Artis Royal Zoo

1:00 pm: Eat lunch at Dewaterkant

3:00 pm: Leave ARTIS Royal Zoo

3:45 pm: Arrive at Vondelpark

4:30 pm: Arrive at Kinderkookkafe after exploring Vondelpark

5:30 pm: Leave Kinderkookkafe and return to the hotel by train

6:15 pm: Arrive back at hotel. Eat dinner at hotel and relax

Day 3

7:00 am: Wake up and get ready

7:30 am: Take tram to NEMO Science Museum

8:15 am: Arrive at NEMO Science Museum

12:00 pm: Leave NEMO and return to hotel

12:30 pm: Arrive back at hotel. Eat early lunch and pack

1:00 pm: Check out and take tram to Eurostar terminal

1:30 pm: Arrive at Eurostar terminal

2:00 pm: Board Eurostar to London

6:00 pm: Arrive in London
