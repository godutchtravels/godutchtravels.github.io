Red Plan – 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1

7:16 am: Depart London St Pancras via Eurostar

11:20 am: Arrive at Amsterdam Centraal

11:30 am: Take tram 26 to Four Elements Hotel, IJburg

12:15 pm: Check-in and freshen up

1:30 pm: Lunch at Four Elements Hotel Restaurant

3:00 pm: Stroll and explore IJburg Bay

5:30 pm: Return to hotel

6:00 pm: Dinner at Four Elements Hotel Restaurant

7:00 pm: Relax and rest at the hotel

Day 2

8:00 am: Breakfast at hotel

9:00 am: Take tram to Rijksmuseum (approx. 45 mins)

10:00 am: Visit Rijksmuseum

12:15 pm: Lunch at Museumplein Café

1:30 pm: Visit Van Gogh Museum

3:30 pm: Return to hotel

5:30 pm: Dinner at Four Elements Hotel Restaurant

Day 3

8:00 am: Breakfast at hotel

9:00 am: Travel to Anne Frank House (approx. 45 mins)

10:00 am: Visit Anne Frank House

12:00 pm: Lunch at nearby café

1:30 pm: Guided Bike Tour (Hidden Gems & Highlights – 2.5 hours)

4:00 pm: Finish tour and return to hotel

6:00 pm: Dinner at Four Elements Hotel Restaurant

Day 4

7:00 am: Breakfast at hotel

8:00 am: Travel to Zaanse Schans (approx. 1.5 hours)

9:30 am: Explore Zaanse Schans

12:00 pm: Lunch at local eatery

1:00 pm: Travel to Keukenhof Gardens (approx. 1.5 hours)

2:30 pm: Visit Keukenhof Gardens

5:30 pm: Return to hotel

7:30 pm: Dinner at Four Elements Hotel Restaurant

Day 5

8:00 am: Breakfast at hotel

9:00 am: Check out and travel to Amsterdam Centraal

11:20 am: Depart Amsterdam via Eurostar

3:30 pm: Arrive at London St Pancras
